Classic Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Eggs 2
Sugar 2 tbsp
Butter 2 tbsp (melted)

Step 1: In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and a pinch of salt.

Step 2: Whisk in milk, eggs, and melted butter until smooth.

Step 3: Heat a non-stick skillet and pour in batter to form pancakes. Cook until golden brown on each side.

Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Eggs 4
Parmesan Cheese 100g
Pancetta 150g
Black Pepper to taste

Step 1: Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.

Step 2: In a bowl, whisk eggs and mix with grated Parmesan.

Step 3: Cook pancetta in a skillet until crispy. Add spaghetti to the skillet.

Step 4: Remove skillet from heat and stir in the egg mixture. Add black pepper to taste and serve.

Source: Simply Recipes
